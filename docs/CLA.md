## Why a CLA at all

llm-redact is free software (GNU AGPL-3.0) with a dual-licensed business
model: the same maintainer also ships the proprietary `llm-redact-pro`
package, which is a derivative of this core. That model only works while
the maintainer holds sufficient rights in the whole core. This agreement
grants those rights **without taking yours away**: you keep full
ownership of your contribution and may use it anywhere else, under any
license, forever. Your contribution is and will remain available to
everyone under the AGPL-3.0 — this agreement additionally lets the
maintainer license it on other terms (which is what keeps the pro
package, and any future license adjustment, legally possible without
tracking down every past contributor).

## How agreement is indicated

Stated in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md): every commit in a pull
request must carry a `Signed-off-by:` line (`git commit -s`) with your
real name and email, which for this project indicates agreement to this
CLA (not only the Developer Certificate of Origin) for the changes in
that pull request. Trivial changes (typo fixes, whitespace) may be
accepted without it at the Maintainer's discretion.
